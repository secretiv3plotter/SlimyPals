Slimy Pals by J4M 

Concept:
Users raise slime pets.
When friends are online, you can see their domain with their own pets and interact with them. 
Interactions: 
    poke (triggers slime bounce) realtime
    feed (friends can help feed your slimes and vice versa) realtime
A user can only have 4 friends.

To raise slime pets you have to:
Summon baby slimes from the summoning ground 
    Different rarities of slimes can be summoned
        Common (3 types) - 60% (20% each)
        Rare (3 types) - 35% (11.66% each)
        Mythical (3 types) - 5% (1.66% each)
	Each slime summoned also has assigned random color (because the default slime sprite is in grayscale)
Users can only summon 9 slimes every day. 
Users can only have 25 max slimes in their domain.
Users can remove a slime.
Make slime food only once every day (reset at 12 AM).
    There is a slime food factory.
    The factory can only produce as much as the amount of current slimes in the domain.
    Food can be stacked if not used.
	Max food stock is 100.
Raise the slimes by feeding them slime food.
    Feeding will be required every 6 hours (last time fed) until max level.
    1 slime food = 1 feed
Feeding increases their level
    Level 1 - baby
    Level 2 - teen
    Level 3 - adult (max)

Core Features:
Summon slimes
Make slime food
Feed slimes
Add 4 friends and see their yards if you all are online
Interact with your friends’ slimes by poking or feeding them
